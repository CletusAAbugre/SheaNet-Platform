The Challenge & Opportunity

The Problem

Local shea butter producers, especially women, struggle with:

  • Limited market access & lack of buyers
  • No investment opportunities
  • Financial constraints & outdated processing methods
  • Exploitation by middlemen
  • Lack of modern equipment and training

The Opportunity

Global shea butter market is projected to reach $3.5 billion by 2027, growing at 6.8% CAGR according to a report by Polaris Market Research.

  • Growing demand for natural, organic skincare products
  • Increasing awareness of fair trade and ethical sourcing
  • Ghana is the #2 producer of shea nuts globally
  • Untapped potential in premium, traceable shea products
  • Strong social impact potential through women's empowerment
